About

Joseph Chaudhari is the Founder and CEO of Proponti. He has been involved business management, acquisition, and sales since 2010. He has a background in nuclear power and has co-founded various "lightning" ventures such as TeliTire, Joe's Garbage, and Green for Green. He holds an MBA from UNC Chapel Hill, a Master's Degree in Structural Engineering from Drexel University, and completed undergraduate studies in Nuclear and Civil Engineering at Drexel University. Joseph has also completed Cornell's Project Management 360 Program, Harvard's Negotiation Mastery Program, and is currently enrolled in The University of Texas at Austin's Full Stack Development Program. Joseph believes in constanly learning new materials so that he can provide his clients with the most up-to-date and holistic business solutions.
